About Me!

Hi, I’m Abby Malakoff — a writer, data analyst, and aspiring book publisher currently completing my senior year at the University of Massachusetts Amherst. I'm majoring in English and Business, with a focus in Professional Writing and Technical Communication.

 

My academic and creative interests center around storytelling, data-driven decision making, and accessibility in publishing. I’m particularly passionate about using analytics to improve reader engagement and create inclusive content experiences.

Outside the classroom, I’ve worked on editorial teams, built digital portfolios, and created visual work in a number of Adobe platforms. This website was made with MadCap Flare and I also created another website fully coded by me using HTML/CSS that will showcase my creative writing, academic projects, and software skills.

Looking ahead, I am pursuing a master’s degree in Business Analytics and hope to eventually launch my own publishing company: one that’s innovative, inclusive, and reader-focused.
