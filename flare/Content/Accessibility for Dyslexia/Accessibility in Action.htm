Accessibility in Action: Audio & User Feedback Challenge

Objective:Create alternative content formats and test your digital material with real users to evaluate accessibility for individuals with dyslexia.

Task: Create an Audio or Text-to-Speech Version

Tool Options: Natural Reader, Google Text-to-Speech, Microsoft Read Aloud, or audio recording software

Instructions:

  1. Choose a short section of content you’ve created (e.g., an article, data summary, or story).

  2. Use a text-to-speech tool or record yourself reading it aloud.

  3. Upload or embed the audio in your document or presentation format (e.g., in Google Slides, PDFs, or web platforms).

  4. Test it: Play it back to check clarity, pacing, and if the content is easy to follow without visuals.

*Bonus: Offer both a slower and regular-speed version for user preference.*
